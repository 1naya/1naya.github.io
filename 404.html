迷途之人，何至此间？昔日月明，今何所至？
若欲留坐，亦可一叙；若问前路，我可指君。前路漫漫，勿失本心。
Lost traveler, how comest thou to this place?
Once bright the moon, now where dost thou roam?
If thou wish to stay, we may share grace,
If thou seek the way, I shall guide thee home.
The road ahead is long, with shadows cast,
In every twist and turn, strange sights await.
Yet guard thy heart, for dangers come at last,
Lest thou forget what makes thy spirit great.
So linger not in wonders far and wide,
For fleeting moments steal thy time away.
Embrace the journey, let thy soul abide,
Yet hold to truth, and keep thy fears at bay.
欲往何处？
 Where shall we go?
